The entrepreneur should invest in advertising on a target group group. This is because from the analysis we have observed that the younger generation takes more time compare to the middle aged individuls but their rate of clicking the app is minial. Thus, she should target the middle aged people. I have also observed that the most gender that clicked the ad are the females who happen to spend more time on the ad compared to males.
